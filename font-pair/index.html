ARTICLE PREVIEW

Design Principles for Modern Web Applications

Typography is one of the most important aspects of web design. The fonts you choose can dramatically affect how your content is perceived by users. A well-chosen font pairing creates visual hierarchy and improves readability.

When selecting font combinations, consider the contrast between headings and body text. Serif fonts often work well for headings, while sans-serif fonts provide clean readability for body content. The key is finding balance between visual interest and legibility.
