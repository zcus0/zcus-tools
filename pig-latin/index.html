Pig Latin Rules

  1. If a word begins with a vowel (a, e, i, o, u), add "way" (or "yay") to the end: apple → appleway
  2. If a word begins with a consonant, move consonants to the end and add "ay": hello → ellohay
  3. For consonant clusters, move all consonants before the first vowel: string → ingstray
  4. Capitalize words maintain their capitalization: Hello → Ellohay
  5. Punctuation is preserved after the word
